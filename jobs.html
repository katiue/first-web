Data Scientist

Description

You will take a lead role in building visibility into what impacts AI is having in the world today and developing tools to forecast future impacts.

Annual Salary Range

$245,000—$310,000 USD

Key Responsibilities

  1. Embed with partners to set-up and carry-out experiments.
  2. Define and interpret A/B tests that help answer critical questions about the impact of model and UX changes to our product.
  3. Develop and socialize dashboards, reports, and other ways of enabling the team and company to answer policy- and impact-relevant.
  4. Create tools to make our data and findings more accessible for external stakeholders.
  5. Run campaigns to collect and validate findings with external stakeholders.

Required Qualifications

  • Essential:
    • 5+ years experience in a quantitative role navigating highly ambiguous environments, ideally as a data scientist or research economist
    • Proposed, designed, and run rigorous experiments with clear insights utilizing SQL and Python
    • Demonstrated prior experience in NLP, large language models, or generative AI
  • Preferable:
    • Experience validating quantitative insights with qualitative methods (e.g. surveys, UXR)
    • Experience in government or policy-adjacent worlds a plus

NLP Engineer

Description

  2. Train and tune GPT-3 models and potentially other NLP/NLU models

  • Preferable:
